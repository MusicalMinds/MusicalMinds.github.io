Musical Minds

After-School Program

At Musical Minds, our mission is to inspire and empower children through the transformative power of music. We are dedicated to helping children develop a lifelong love of music while fostering creativity, self-expression, and discipline. Our unique program combines music education with language learning, providing an immersive environment where children can not only explore the world of music but also enhance their English language skills. Through engaging and interactive lessons, we aim to create a nurturing and inclusive community that encourages personal growth, cultural understanding, and a deep appreciation for the arts.

About Us

Miss Julie

Instruments - Voice, Piano, & Guitar

Julie has been teaching as a professional educator since 2016 and holds a Teaching Degree from Utah State University as well as extensive training in Music Therapy.

As a seasoned vocal instructor and elementary educator, Julie brings a wealth of experience and expertise to her teaching practice. Her specialty is in teaching singers to sing in a healthy way that will protect their voice. Her passion for music and dedication to helping students develop a love for music is evident in her teaching approach.

Miss Paula

Instrument - Violin

As an experienced Violin instructor, Paula brings HOW MANY years of teaching experience to her students. She holds a degree in Music from Utah State University and is a current member of the Orchestra.

Paula's extensive knowledge of music theory combined with her dedication to helping students learn and appreciate music sets her apart as a passionate and skilled educator. With her background and commitment to excellence, Paula is devoted to nurturing the musical talents of her students and instilling in them a love for music that will last a lifetime.
